# State-Space Worked Example

## Problem

Given:

$$
A=\begin{bmatrix}0&1\\-2&-3\end{bmatrix},\quad
B=\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\end{bmatrix},\quad
C=\begin{bmatrix}1&0\end{bmatrix}
$$

Check controllability and observability.

## Solution

### Step 1: Controllability matrix

$$
\mathcal{C}=[B\ AB]
$$

Compute $AB$:

$$
AB=
\begin{bmatrix}0&1\\-2&-3\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}0\\1\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}1\\-3\end{bmatrix}
$$

So:

$$
\mathcal{C}=
\begin{bmatrix}
0 & 1 \\
1 & -3
\end{bmatrix}
$$

$$
\det(\mathcal{C})=(0)(-3)-(1)(1)=-1\neq0
$$

Rank is 2, so system is controllable.

### Step 2: Observability matrix

$$
\mathcal{O}=
\begin{bmatrix}
C \\
CA
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Compute $CA$:

$$
CA=
\begin{bmatrix}1&0\end{bmatrix}
\begin{bmatrix}0&1\\-2&-3\end{bmatrix}
=
\begin{bmatrix}0&1\end{bmatrix}
$$

Thus:

$$
\mathcal{O}=
\begin{bmatrix}
1&0\\
0&1
\end{bmatrix}
$$

Rank is 2, so system is observable.

## Final answer

The given realization is both controllable and observable.
